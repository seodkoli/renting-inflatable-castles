About Pflugerville, Texas

Pflugerville (FLOO-gÉ™É™ r-vil) is a city in Travis County, Texas, USA, with a small portion in Williamson County. The populace was 65,191 at the 2020 census. Pflugerville is a suburb of Austin and part of the Austin–-- Round Rock–-- Metropolitan Statistical Area. It was called after the initial German inhabitants who farmed the location; Pflüger means "plowman".
