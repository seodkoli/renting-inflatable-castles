About Pflugerville, Texas

Pflugerville (FLOO-gÉ™É™ r-vil) is a city in Travis County, Texas, United States, with a small portion in Williamson Region. The population was 65,191 at the 2020 census. Pflugerville is a suburb of Austin and component of the Austin–-- Round Rock–-- Metropolitan Statistical Location. It was called after the initial German inhabitants that farmed the area; Pflüger implies "plowman".
