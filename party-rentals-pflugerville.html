About Pflugerville, Texas

Pflugerville (FLOO-gÉ™É™ r-vil) is a city in Travis Area, Texas, United States, with a tiny part in Williamson Region. The population was 65,191 at the 2020 census. Pflugerville is a suburban area of Austin and component of the Austin–-- Round Rock–-- Metropolitan Statistical Area. It was named after the initial German inhabitants who farmed the area; Pflüger implies "plowman".
