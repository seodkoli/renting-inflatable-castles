About Pflugerville, Texas

Pflugerville (FLOO-gÉ™É™ r-vil) is a city in Travis Region, Texas, United States, with a little portion in Williamson County. The populace was 65,191 at the 2020 census. Pflugerville is a suburb of Austin and component of the Austin–-- Round Rock–-- Metropolitan Statistical Location. It was called after the initial German settlers that farmed the area; Pflüger suggests "plowman".
