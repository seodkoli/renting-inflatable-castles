About Pflugerville, Texas

Pflugerville (FLOO-gÉ™É™ r-vil) is a city in Travis Area, Texas, USA, with a tiny portion in Williamson Area. The population was 65,191 at the 2020 demographics. Pflugerville is a suburb of Austin and part of the Austin–-- Round Rock–-- Metropolitan Statistical Area. It was called after the original German settlers who farmed the area; Pflüger suggests "plowman".
