About Pflugerville, Texas

Pflugerville (FLOO-gÉ™É™ r-vil) is a city in Travis Region, Texas, United States, with a little section in Williamson County. The population was 65,191 at the 2020 census. Pflugerville is a suburban area of Austin and component of the Austin–-- Round Rock–-- Metropolitan Statistical Area. It was called after the original German settlers who farmed the area; Pflüger indicates "plowman".
